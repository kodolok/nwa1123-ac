• FW Version: ~`cat /etc/firmware_name`~
  • Serial Number: ~`webget_factory_serial_number`~
  • 2.4G SSID: ~`webget_factory_ssid1`~
  • 2.4G Date rate: ~`webget_factory_data_rate1`~
  • 2.4G Channel: ~`webget_factory_channel1`~
  • 5G SSID: ~`webget_factory_ssid2`~
  • 5G Date rate: ~`webget_factory_data_rate2`~
  • 5G Channel: ~`webget_factory_channel2`~
  • Eth0 MAC: ~`get_if_hwaddr eth0`~
  • Wifi0 MAC: ~`get_if_hwaddr wifi0 | cut -c1-17`~
  • Wifi1 MAC: ~`get_if_hwaddr wifi1 | cut -c1-17`~
  • Region: ~`webget_factory_region`~
  • Coexist: ~`webget_factory_coexist`~
